# Demo Script

## Goal

Tell a clear product story in under 3 minutes.

## Recommended Flow

### 1. Open Dashboard

Show:

- policies in force
- claims awaiting review
- premium collection status
- AI triage overview

Narrative:
This is a cyber insurance operations workspace for teams that need policy visibility and faster claims handling.

### 2. Open Claims View

Show:

- a newly reported claim
- reserve amount
- status
- attached or expected documents

Narrative:
Claims teams can immediately see what was reported, how severe it is, and what is missing.

### 3. Show AI Triage

Show:

- claim priority
- reason tags
- recommendation

Narrative:
Instead of generic AI, the product gives an explainable prioritization layer so adjusters know what to act on first.

### 4. Show Policy Context

Show:

- policy coverage
- premium
- renewal date
- linked claims

Narrative:
The claim is not isolated. The adjuster and broker can see full policy context in one place.

### 5. Close With Business Value

Use lines like:

- faster claims triage
- better visibility across policy and claims data
- cleaner broker and policyholder experience
- faster deployment than heavy legacy insurance systems

## Demo Rule

Do not try to show every feature. Show one end-to-end story cleanly.
